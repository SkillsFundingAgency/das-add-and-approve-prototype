What you'll need

If an apprentice you're training needs to move to a new employer, you should:

  • speak to the new employer to agree new training dates and price
  • request permission to move the apprentice to their apprenticeship service account

Once you've agreed these details, you can change the employer.

Where an apprentice is subject to a funding reservation, moving the apprentice to a new employer will not require a new reservation, or use a reservation from the new employer's limit.
